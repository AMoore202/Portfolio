CONTEXT:

The national health care sector in Canada is inherently complex. Given that health care delivery is provincial jurisdiction, national health is primarily focused on higher level goals, namely transforming how health is conceived and executed across the country. In addition to Health Canada, this transformation is the responsibility of a handful of non-profit organizations called Pan-Canadian Health Organizations (PCHOs). Although each PCHO has a unique mandate, there are also large areas of overlap. In order to do it's best work, it is crucial that PCHOs continuously refine their core purpose and identify where they can make this biggest. As part of a strategic planning process, my team's job was to facilitate a PCHO in updating their purpose.
